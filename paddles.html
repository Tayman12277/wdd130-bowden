Selkirk

Selkirk is one of the most popular and trusted pickleball brands. They are known for their premium materials, great control, and long-lasting construction. Many competitive players use their paddles.

JOOLA

JOOLA makes powerful, spin-friendly paddles. They became extremely popular after partnering with pro player Ben Johns. Their paddles often use carbon fiber and advanced technology for better performance.

Onix

ONix is widely used among casual and competitive players. Their paddles tend to be powerful and durable, making them good for hard hitters and players who like aggressive shots.

CRBN

CRBN paddles are known for their carbon fiber surfaces that create excellent spin. Many advanced players love this brand for its blend of power and control.

Friday

Friday is known for offering affordable, high-quality paddles with carbon fiber surfaces and good spin. Their paddles are USA Pickleball approved and come witha 99=day happiness guarantee. They're popular for being lightweight, grippy, and budget-friendly.

Ronbus

Ronbus is growing their pickleball brand known for its raw carbon fiber surfaces and advanced engineering. Their paddles offer great spin, solid control, and a modern thermoformed design similar to high-end paddles - but usually at a more affordable price. Players like Ronbus for its quality, durability and value.
